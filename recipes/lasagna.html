Lasagna Recipes

A perfect lasagna recipe

An ultimate recipe for any occasion whether it's for a special event, a family gathering or just for a casual dinner, it never disappoints! Who wouldnt love some good fashioned Italian meal?

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but it's well worth it! Without furder ado, let's look into what ingredients are needed for us to make this fancy and delicious food at home!

Ingredients:

  • Meat: We will use a Lean ground beef for this recipe
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are added to the meat to add a nice flavor
  • Tomato products: We are going to be using a can of cushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and at last, two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar, to add subtle sweetness to the flavour of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: We can use a pre-made store bought lasagna noodle, or we can use our own homemade.
  • Cheeses: A mix of Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese will make this lasagna extra indulging.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.
