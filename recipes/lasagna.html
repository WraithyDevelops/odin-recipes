Lasagna


Prep time Cook time Servings
30 mins 2h 30mins 12

A perfect lasagna recipe

An ultimate recipe for any occasion whether it's for a special event, a family gathering or just for a casual dinner, it never disappoints! Who wouldn't love some good fashioned Italian meal? Making lasagna can be time consuming, but it's well worth it! Without further ado, let's look into what ingredients are needed for us to make this fancy and delicious food at home!

Ingredients:

  • Meat: ¾lb Lean ground beef & 1lb sweet Italian sausage
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic for added flavor
  • Tomato products: A can of crushed tomatoes(28 oz.), two cans of tomato sauce(6 ½ oz.) and at last, two cans of tomato paste (6 oz.)
  • Sugar: 2 tbsp of white sugar, to add subtle sweetness to the flavour of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: 4 tbsp of Fresh parsley, 1 ½ tsp of dried basil leaves, 1 ½ tsp of salt, 1 tsp of italian seasoning, ½ tsp of fennel seeds and ¼ tsp of ground black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: 12 lasagna noodles - either store bought or homemade.
  • Cheeses: A mix of Parmesan(¾ c.), mozzarella(¾ lb), and ricotta cheese(16 oz.) as the perfect trio to make it extra indulging
  • Egg: An egg to help bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Now that we are done with our grocery list, let's begin Step-By-Step:

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
