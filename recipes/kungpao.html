Kung Pao Chicken


Prep time Cook time Servings
30 mins 30 mins 4

Restaurant-worthy Kung Pao Chicken at home

This tasty kung pao chicken is similar to what is served in Chinese restaurants. It's easy to make, and you can be as creative with the measurements as you want.
You can't go wrong! The sauce reduces until nice and thick.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts: Cubed chicken breasts
  • Vegetables: Onion, Garlic, Peanuts, Chestnuts
  • Marinade: Made with cornstarch, water, white wine, soy sauce, and sesame oil
  • Sweet-savory sauce: Made with hot chili paste, brown sugar, white vinegar

Now that we are done with our grocery list, let's begin Step-By-Step:

  1. Make the marinade, then marinate chicken in the fridge.
  2. Make the chili paste-brown sugar sauce. Add the remaining ingredients and cook.
  3. Cook the marinated chicken in a skillet until it's cooked through.
  4. Add the chicken to the chili paste-brown sugar sauce.
  5. Continue cooking until the sauce thickens.
